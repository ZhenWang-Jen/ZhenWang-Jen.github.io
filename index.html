About Zhen Wang

Zhen Wang is an honor graduate from Arizona State University with her bachelor's degree in Computer Information Systems. After graduation, Zhen works as an IT consultant, and a UI & UX professional for different clients across industries.

Zhen is focused on creating product solutions for the Information Technology industry. Following lean & agile methdologies while leveraging the fundamentals of the design process, Zhen ensures the value to you is a more credible, user-centric, and functional product solution.
