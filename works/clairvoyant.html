As one of the main developers who works on clairvoyantsoft.com, I integrate the Bullhorn’s APIs to help hire more talents for Clairvoyant LLC.

The Open Source Career Portal (OSCP) is a free customizable job portal that gives job seekers access to your company's published jobs.

PROJECT BACKGROUND

Clairvoyant is a Software Engineering company with specialized skills for Big Data, Managed Services, Enterprise IT Services, and Design Services. These specialized branches have developed throughout the years to create a compelling offering to the IT Enterprise Servicing industry, making Clairvoyant a shop that can deliver products and services across these varied pillars.

Throughout this evolution of service pillars, the individual brands within Clairvoyant have developed into their own direction. This is not a problem for the individual brand, however, it is harder to pitch Clairvoyant as a unified company. The purpose of this project is to refactor the brands so that they portray a unified and strong offering while making it easier to understand one and all with a single unified message.

PROJECT GOALS

To upgrade the Clairvoyant website with the new design which becomes easier for the public to know Clairvoyant and its services through brand artifacts. Also, to integrate ways for fast-moving careers postings that allow candidates to apply directly through the website.

Acceptance Criteria

  • To retrieve all the jobs that are published on Bullhorn.
  • To filter through available jobs by locations and categories.
  • To view the job detail of any of the jobs.
  • To validate application form information.
  • The implementation of Google reCAPTCHA to stop bots.
  • To send any validated appliation to Bullhorn platform with a valid resume attached.
  • To show proper messages to candidates for successful or failed job submissions.
  • The ability to navigate back to other Clairvoyant pages.
